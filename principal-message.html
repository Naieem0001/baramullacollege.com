Message from the Principal

It gives me immense pleasure to extend a warm welcome to all learners, parents, alumni and stakeholders of Government Degree College Baramulla (Autonomous). Our institution stands on a legacy of academic commitment, ethical values and social engagement, and continues to evolve with the changing needs of higher education and society.

At GDC Baramulla, we strive to nurture not only competent graduates but also responsible citizens who are equipped with critical thinking, creativity, digital competence and a deep sense of social responsibility. Our academic environment encourages innovation, interdisciplinary learning and community participation, preparing our students for the dynamic global landscape.

Professional Career & Leadership

Prof. Mohammad Shafi Lone is a distinguished academic administrator in Jammu and Kashmir and is currently serving as the Principal of Government Degree College Baramulla (Autonomous), having assumed charge on 1st July 2024.

GDC Baramulla (Autonomous) – Current Assignment

Under his leadership, the college has actively strengthened its academic and outreach ecosystem. The institution successfully organised a five-day Cybersecurity Bootcamp in December 2025, promoting digital safety, technical skills and awareness among students. Several initiatives focusing on entrepreneurship development and organ donation awareness have also been introduced to encourage social responsibility and innovation-driven learning.

GDC Kupwara – Former Principal

Prior to joining GDC Baramulla, Prof. Lone served as the Principal of Government Degree College Kupwara. During his tenure, the institution achieved the prestigious NAAC ‘A’ Grade accreditation, reflecting significant improvements in academic standards, governance, student support services and institutional practices.

In recognition of this achievement, he was honoured by the Lieutenant Governor of Jammu and Kashmir, Shri Manoj Sinha, in November 2022.

GDC Sogam (Lolab) – Previous Assignment

Prof. Lone also served as the Principal of Government Degree College Sogam (Lolab). During this period, he played a key role in strengthening academic administration, infrastructure planning and student-centric programmes before being transferred to Kupwara in August 2022.

Vision for GDC Baramulla

The vision of Prof. Mohammad Shafi Lone is to transform GDC Baramulla into a nationally respected autonomous institution that blends academic rigour with innovation, research orientation, digital transformation and strong community engagement.

Emphasis is placed on outcome-based education, industry-relevant skills, entrepreneurship culture, ethical leadership and inclusive growth. The college aspires to serve as a centre of excellence for multidisciplinary learning and societal development in North Kashmir.

“Education must illuminate minds, empower communities and inspire responsible leadership. At GDC Baramulla, our collective mission is to build a future where knowledge, integrity and innovation walk hand in hand.”

— Prof. Mohammad Shafi Lone
